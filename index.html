About Me

Creative, analytical attentive to detail, and team player are all attributes that best describe and sum up my career as a software developer and executive manager.

Beginning my career as a software developer on mainframe systems, solving the woes of Y2K, my career has run the gamut of legacy to client server systems. I now find myself yearning to join the ranks of exciting web development.

I plan to combine my technical skills with my functional and management skills to build some amazing web applications and tools. I am excited about my new journey!
